Customer relationship management system

Customer relationship management system refers to the use of software, hardware and network technology to establish an information system for enterprises to collect, manage, analyze and utilize customer information. With the management of customer data as the core, it records various interactions between enterprises and customers in the process of marketing and sales, as well as the status of various related activities, and provides various data models to provide support for later analysis and decision-making.

Hardware and software

Software:Shape Software

Shape is purpose-built sales and marketing automation software that comes with pre-built features that can be configured for any business. The easy-to-use interface empowers your teams and consolidates all aspects of your business in one AI platform.

Hardware:NAS

Network Attached Storage is literally a device that is connected to the network and has the function of data storage, so it is also called "Network Storage". It is a dedicated data storage server. It is data-centric, completely separates storage devices from servers, and centrally manages data, thereby freeing bandwidth, improving performance, reducing total cost of ownership, and protecting investments. Its cost is much lower than using server storage, and its efficiency is much higher.

Development steps

The basic steps in developing a CRM system should include the following:

1. Create a plan The establishment of the CRM system needs to be combined with the actual situation of the enterprise and supported by various resources. Therefore, before implementation, it is necessary to accurately grasp the application requirements of the enterprise and formulate a high-level business plan that combines technical solutions and enterprise resources.

2. Form a dedicated team It is an important guarantee to ensure the smooth progress of the system by drawing competent representatives from each department that intends to use the CRM system to form a team. Therefore, after the plan is determined, the team should be formed in time and carry out early concept promotion and training.

3. Analyze customer needs and initially build the system Because the main purpose of establishing a CRM system is to improve customer satisfaction and increase corporate benefits, it is necessary to analyze customer needs, deeply understand the different service requirements of different customer groups, and find the interaction between companies and customers to ensure the economy and practicality of customer files. sex. Enterprises should establish different customer file contents according to the characteristics of customers, and establish a preliminary customer information management system.

4. Clarify enterprise application requirements After understanding the customer's needs, it is necessary to analyze, evaluate and reconstruct the business process of the enterprise on this basis, and re-establish a new business process. This process requires extensive consultation with employees and the full participation of lower-level employees and managers to ensure that the system can perform the various functions required by the end-user employees.

5. Determine the appropriate plan, coordinate resources, and advance in stages The promotion of CRM solutions is not achieved overnight, but should be implemented gradually in a gradual manner, so that enterprises can find problems at any time and fully understand and solve them, and can also adjust at any time according to business needs. It must be emphasized that new functions should be gradually added as needed, which is not only more applicable, but also avoids the confusion on the system caused by a one-time increase.

6. Training In order to ensure the successful application of the CRM system and enable the users of the system to master the use method as soon as possible, it is necessary to carry out timely training. The training objects should mainly include enterprise management personnel, sales personnel and service personnel, and the content should include usage methods, precautions and maintenance points.

7. Use, Evaluation and Improvement By using the CRM system, enterprises should gradually give full play to the advantages of the system. In the process of using, the enterprise should evaluate the validity of the system application together with the system developers and suppliers, so as to continuously find problems, revise different modules, and gradually improve its practicability.
